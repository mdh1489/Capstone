About GoLocal

Our Goal

The local community around you is what makes a city thrive and flourish. Small business are having a hard time keeping up with the sales and the outreach of the out of town corporations. Our goal is to give the small businesses a fighting chance.

About the Developer

Hi, my name is Michael Huneycutt and I am a senior majoring in Computer Science at App State University. I wanted to make a difference in the community, at the same time, be able to learn from this project. I grew up in the small town of Lexington, NC. I personally know what it feels like when the community is shaken by large corporations moving in and taking over. The ability to compete with them is very limited because of the amount of funds the small business have compared to the large corporations. That is why I chose to do this project as my Capstone.
